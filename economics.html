Box plot — log scale helps reveal structure hidden by extreme outliers

Insights

  • Net worth is highly skewed: a small number of individuals sit far above the typical billionaire.
  • Log scaling makes mid-range differences visible instead of being dominated by the richest outliers.
  • The distribution supports the idea that “billionaire” is a wide category, not a single wealth level.

Institutional Quality & Billionaire Wealth

This page examines whether stronger institutions (lower corruption) are associated with greater billionaire wealth and concentration.

Corruption Perception Index vs Total Billionaire Wealth

Scatter relationship

• Countries with stronger institutions tend to accumulate more billionaire wealth.
• Corruption alone does not prevent billionaire formation, but it affects scale.
• Institutional quality amplifies long-term wealth accumulation.

Top 15 Countries by Billionaire Count

Comparison view

• High-count countries cluster around moderate-to-strong institutional scores.
• Few high-corruption countries sustain large billionaire populations.
• Institutions appear more important for *retaining* wealth than creating it.

Gender Inequality Among Billionaires

This section explores gender disparities in billionaire representation, wealth distribution, and geographic variation.

Gender Distribution of Billionaires

Donut chart

• The billionaire population is overwhelmingly male.
• Women represent only a small fraction of global extreme wealth holders.
• Gender inequality persists even at the highest economic levels.

Billionaire Net Worth by Gender

Log-scale box plot

• Wealth is highly skewed for both genders.
• Female billionaires span a wide wealth range despite lower representation.
• Inequality exists both *between* and *within* genders.

Female Share of Billionaires by Country

Normalized comparison

• Female representation varies significantly across countries.
• Some nations outperform others relative to size.
• Cultural and institutional factors likely play a role.
